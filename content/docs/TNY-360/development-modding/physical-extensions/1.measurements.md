# Mount measurements

The best way to add custom extensions to your TNY-360 is to use the universal mount located on the back of the robot.

In order to help you design your extensions for a perfect fit, here are some detailed measurements of the universal mount and its surroundings.

---


::info-box{title="Clearance" type="tip"}
Nothing is perfect, and 3D prints can have some variability.<br>
The universal mount already has a built-in clearance of 0.2mm on all sides, but if you want to be extra sure, we recommend adding an additional clearance of 0.4mm in your designs.
::
