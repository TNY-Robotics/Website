# Practical Guide

Welcome on the TNY-360 Practical Guide !

This section is designed to help you get started with your TNY-360, regardless of your experience level and the type of TNY-360 kit you have *(even if you don't have one at all)*.

---

::

::chevron

## ⚠️ Critical Safety Guidelines

::info-box{title="DANGER: LiPo Battery Safety" type="danger"}
Lithium-Polymer (LiPo) batteries present serious dangers, including the output of high currents and a severe risk of fire if mishandled or punctured. Always handle, charge, and store your power source with extreme caution.
::

::info-box{title="Battery Supply Information" type="info"}
The standard TNY-360 kit is supplied without a battery. We recommend using a standard RC 3S LiPo battery with an XT30 connector.<br>Alternatively, your specific kit may have been shipped with an [experimental prototype battery pack](../anatomy/electronics/battery-pack.md).
::
