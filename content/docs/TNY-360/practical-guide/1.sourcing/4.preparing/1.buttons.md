# The Buttons

We'll start by soldering the connectors for the two buttons of the robot !

---

## The connection

::split{side="right"}
#left
The connections are pretty straight forward, since the connectors use the same pin order as the buttons :

1. **GND**: The ground. *(which is the pin most to the left)*
2. **I/O**: The signal pin. *(which is the pin in the middle)*
3. **VCC**: The power supply. *(which is the pin most to the right)*

*(don't pay attention to the color of the wires in the picture, they are not standardized)*

#right
<img src="/docs/images/comp-prep/button.jpg" class="w-full" />
::

An ideal length for the button cables is around **5cm to 10cm**, so you get some slack to connect the buttons easily,
while avoiding having a lot of excess cable inside the head when assembled.

---

Once the two buttons are soldered to their connectors, you can move on to the next step !
