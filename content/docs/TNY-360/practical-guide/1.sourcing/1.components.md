## 3 - Get the Screws and Cables

The TNY-360 uses a couple of M2.5 screws and heat inserts for assembly, as well as various JST-PH and JST-XH cables for PCB connections.

As with the components, a raw list of all the screws, heat inserts and cables can be found in the [GitHub repository](https://github.com/TNY-Robotics/TNY-360/tree/main/BOM),
but if you want to build your own TNY-360 without having to go through the hassle of picking each item separately, the best option is to get the following kits containing all the necessary screws, heat inserts and cables in one package:

| Item                  | Description                                                  | Version                             | Link                                                               |
