# Wiring the Leg

Now that the leg is fully assembled and attached to the body, let's wire it up !

---

## Wiring the leg connectors

All the leg connectors (motors and contact switch) will be connected to the Paw Board in the center of the torso.

### The Contact Switch

::step
#animation
<Animation src="/docs/images/assembly/leg-bl/wiring/switch.webm" />

#text
**Plug the contact switch connector into the `JST-PH 2-Pin` port** of the Paw Board, with the :logo{name="paw"} Paw logo next to it.

Make sure you **plug the connector in the left side** of the board to avoid switching the left and right sensors.
::

### The Hip-Pitch Motor

::step
#animation
<Animation src="/docs/images/assembly/leg-bl/wiring/hip.webm" />

#text
**Plug the Hip-Pitch motor connector into the second `JST-XH 4-Pin` port** of the Paw Board, with the **HP** label next to it.

Make sure you **plug the connector in the left side** of the board to avoid switching the left and right motors.
::

### The Knee-Pitch Motor

::step
#animation
<Animation src="/docs/images/assembly/leg-bl/wiring/knee.webm" />

#text
**Plug the Knee-Pitch motor connector into the last `JST-XH 4-Pin` port** of the Paw Board, with the **KP** label next to it.

Make sure you **plug the connector in the left side** of the board to avoid switching the left and right motors.
::

---

All good ? Let's switch to the right side and start assembling the third leg, then !
