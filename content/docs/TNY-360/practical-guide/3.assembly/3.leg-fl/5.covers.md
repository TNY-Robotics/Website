# The Plastic Covers

*Clip clip clip !*

We'll finish the leg assembly by adding the plastic covers. Have fun snapping them in place,<br>
and don't forget to admire your work once you're done :)

---

## Add the Top Cover

We'll first add the top cover to the bottom cover to get a little bit more style points on this leg !

For this step, you'll need :

| Ressource                     | Quantity |
| ----------------------------- | :------: |
| `Top-Cover (L)` part          |    1     |
| Plastic Clips *(+ Clip Tool)* |    3     |

### Press the Plastic Clips on the Top Cover

::step
#animation
<Animation src="/docs/images/assembly/leg-fl/covers/top-cover-clips.webm" />

#text
Use the Clip Tool to **snap three Plastic Clips into the holes** on the `Top-Cover (L)` part as shown in the video.

Make sure you **push the clips straight** to avoid damaging the clips or the part.
::

### Press the Top Cover on the Bottom Cover

::step
#animation
<Animation src="/docs/images/assembly/leg-fl/covers/top-cover-assembly.webm" />

#text
Now that the clips are in place, **press the `Top-Cover (L)` part on the `Bottom-Cover (L)` part** until it snaps in place.

**Align the ends of the plastic clips** with the holes on the `Bottom-Cover (L)` part, then push the cover straight.
::

---

## Add the Decorations

Even more decorations would be too much ? Never ! Let's add the logo and the stripes to finish this leg in style :)

For this step, you'll need :

| Ressource                     | Quantity |
| ----------------------------- | :------: |
| `Cover-Logo` part             |    1     |
| `Cover-Stripe (L)` part       |    1     |
| Plastic Clips *(+ Clip Tool)* |    4     |

### Press the Plastic Clips on the Logo

::step
#animation
<Animation src="/docs/images/assembly/leg-fl/covers/name.webm" />

#text
We'll start by adding the logo. **Press two Plastic Clips into the holes of the `Cover-Logo` part** as shown in the video.

You know the drill by now, make sure you **push the clips straight** to avoid damaging the clips or the part !
::

### Press the Logo on the Top Cover

::step
#animation
<Animation src="/docs/images/assembly/leg-fl/covers/name.webm" />

#text
Now, **press the `Cover-Logo` part on the `Top-Cover (L)` part** until it snaps in place.
::

### Press the Plastic Clips on the Stripes

::step
#animation
<Animation src="/docs/images/assembly/leg-fl/covers/name.webm" />

#text
Same thing for the stripes, **press two Plastic Clips into the holes of the `Cover-Stripe (L)` part** as shown in the video.

Straight push, straight push, straight push !
::

### Press the Stripes on the Top Cover

::step
#animation
<Animation src="/docs/images/assembly/leg-fl/covers/name.webm" />

#text
Now add the stripes by **pressing the `Cover-Stripe (L)` part on the `Top-Cover (L)` part** until it snaps in place.
::

---

## Add the Bottom Cover

Now that the covers are all done, we'll clip them on the leg and finish this assembly !

For this step, you'll need :

| Ressource                     | Quantity |
| ----------------------------- | :------: |
| `Bottom-Cover (L)` part       |    1     |
| Plastic Clips *(+ Clip Tool)* |    3     |

### Press the Plastic Clips on the Bottom Cover

::step
#animation
<Animation src="/docs/images/assembly/leg-fl/covers/bottom-cover-clips.webm" />

#text
Use the Clip Tool to **snap three Plastic Clips into the holes** on the `Bottom-Cover (L)` part as shown in the video.

Make sure you **push the clips straight** to avoid damaging the clips or the part.
::


### Press the Bottom Cover on the Leg Top Part

::step
#animation
<Animation src="/docs/images/assembly/leg-fl/covers/bottom-cover-assembly.webm" />

#text
Now that the clips are in place, **press the `Bottom-Cover (L)` part on the `Leg-Top (FL)` part** until it sits flush.

**Align the ends of the plastic clips** with the holes on the `Leg-Top (FL)` part, then push the cover straight until it's fully seated.
::
