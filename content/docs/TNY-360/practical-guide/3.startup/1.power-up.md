# Powering Up

Alright ! You have your TNY-360, you have your battery pack, and you are ready to power up your robot for the first time!

---

::in-progress
::

- Ensure robot is on stable flat surface and legs and in the rest position.

- If battery pack : connect to robot, insert fuse and toggle the power switch.
- If custom pack : connect to robot (no fuse or switch).
