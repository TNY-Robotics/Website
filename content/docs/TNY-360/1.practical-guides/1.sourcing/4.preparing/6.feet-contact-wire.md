# The Feet Contact Wire

The feet contact switch has to be connected to the Paw Boards, here's how to make the custom cable for this connection.

---

The switch for the feet contact plugs into a 3-pin Dupont connector for easier assembly,
but the Paw Board uses a JST-PH 2-pin connector.

So we need to make a custom cable that has a 3-pin Dupont connector on one end and a JST-PH 2-pin connector on the other end.

Since this cable needs to go from the center of the robot's torso (where the Paw Boards are located) to the feet,
we recommend a length of **around 47cm to 50cm**. This will be enough to reach the feet while having some slack on each joint.

## The result

::split{side="right"}
#left
Since the feet contact switch plugs into the left and right pins of the Dupont connector,
**we'll connect the left and right wires of the Dupont connector to the two pins of the JST-PH connector**,
and leave the middle pin of the Dupont connector unconnected.

<br>

Use heat-shrink tubing to make sure the two wires are well insulated and won't cause any false-positive readings
when the wires touch each other.
