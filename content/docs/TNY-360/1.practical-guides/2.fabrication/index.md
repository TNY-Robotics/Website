# Fabrication

Welcome to the first step of building your TNY-360 quadruped robot!

This guide is designed specifically for builders starting with the ***Maker Kit*** (hardware only, no 3D printed parts) or people who have already sourced their materials from the [Sourcing & Preparation](../sourcing) section.

---

Here's what you can expect in this section:

- [**Printing Guide**](./printing)<br>
    You'll see how to 3D print the parts of the TNY-360 with the right settings, materials, and orientations to ensure optimal performance and durability.

- [**Calibration Parts**](./calibration-parts)<br>
    You'll learn how to print and use the calibration parts included in the .3MF file to ensure a perfect fit for all the screws and components of the robot, avoiding potential issues during assembly.
    
- [**Heat Inserts Placement**](./heat-inserts)<br>
    You'll see how to place the heat inserts in the 3D printed parts of the TNY-360 to ensure a strong and durable assembly.

---

Alright, let's get started with the fabrication of your TNY-360 robot !
