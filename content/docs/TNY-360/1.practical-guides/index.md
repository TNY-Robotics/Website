# Practical Guide

Welcome on the TNY-360 Practical Guide !

This section is designed to help you get started with your TNY-360, regardless of your experience level and the type of TNY-360 kit you have *(even if you don't have one at all)*.

---

This practical guide is organised into three main sections. Depending on the type of kit you have, you may want to start at a different point in the guide:

::chevron
[Fabrication](./fabrication) - For those who have the ***Barebones kit*** and need guidance for printing the parts.
::

::chevron
[Assembly](./assembly) - For those who have the ***Maker kit*** and need help with the assembly process.
::

::chevron
[Startup & Setup](./startup) - For those who have the ***Ready-to-Run kit*** and want to get started with their TNY-360.
::

For people wanting to build your TNY-360 **from scratch**, you might want to start reading the **Sourcing & Preparation** section, which will list all the components and tools you need to build a TNY-360.

::chevron
[Sourcing & Preparation](./sourcing) - If you want to build your TNY-360 **from scratch**.
::
